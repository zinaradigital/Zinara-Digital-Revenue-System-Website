Cookies Policy

Last updated: January 17, 2025

Note: This cookies policy should be reviewed and completed by legal counsel to ensure compliance with data protection regulations.

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide a better experience and analyze how our site is used.

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly:

  • Session management
  • Security features
  • Basic functionality

Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors use our website:

  • Page views and traffic sources
  • User behavior patterns
  • Performance metrics

We may use Google Analytics or similar services. These have their own privacy policies.

Functional Cookies

These cookies enhance your experience:

  • Remembering your preferences
  • Chat functionality
  • Form completion assistance

Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services that set their own cookies:

  • Analytics providers (e.g., Google Analytics)
  • Communication tools
  • Social media integrations

How to Control Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in several ways:

Browser Settings

Most browsers allow you to:

  • View and delete cookies
  • Block all cookies
  • Block third-party cookies
  • Clear cookies when you close your browser

Opt-Out Tools

You can opt out of analytics cookies:

  • Google Analytics: Google Analytics Opt-out

Impact of Blocking Cookies

If you block cookies, some features may not work properly:

  • Forms may not function correctly
  • You may need to re-enter information
  • Some pages may load slowly

Updates to This Policy

We may update this cookies policy. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
